About

The game The One Ring 2ed, their logos, some of the game rules and images in the character sheet are trademarks of Free League Publishing. See more about this game on the official page: freeleaguepublishing.com

The generation of characters considers the creation rules available in the book The One Ring: Core Rules (segunda edição), and also the complements of Heroic Cultures available in the books The One Ring: Peoples of Wilderland and The One Ring: Rivendell. Regarding random generation, the algorithm tends to make “good” choices while maintaining random criteria to deliver unique characters.
